## 🚀 Business Recommendations

### ✅ Improve Conversion at Signup Stage

* Simplify registration process
* Optimize landing pages (UI/UX improvements)

---

### ✅ Focus on High-Performing Channels

* Allocate more budget to top-performing channels
* Reduce spend on low-performing sources

---

### ✅ Target High-Conversion Segments

* Run personalized campaigns for high-performing job categories
* Use segmentation-based marketing strategies

---

### ✅ Retarget Potential Customers

* Use remarketing strategies (emails, ads)
* Engage users who visited but didn’t sign up

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This analysis highlights critical bottlenecks in the marketing funnel, especially at the **initial conversion stage**. By optimizing user experience, focusing on high-performing channels, and targeting the right customer segments, businesses can significantly improve overall conversion rates and ROI.
